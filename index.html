Lil Peep 1996-2017

"The pioneer of emo rap"



Lil Peep was an American rapper who was one of the key influencers in "Sad" and "Emo" hip hop music.
He drew influence from rock bands of the 2000's and today's trap style hip hop artists to create a unique sound, proving popular with Gen Z fans.

Lil Peep's life

  • November 1996 - Born in Pennsylvania, USA
  • 2013 - Moves to LA to pursue his music career
  • 2015 - Releases his first full mixtape
  • 2016 - forms Gothboiclique and produces/mixes his entire debut album
  • August 2017 - Releases his debut album, "Come Over When You're Sober Part 1"
  • November 2017 - Dies from a Xanax overdose before a performance
